---
title: "Rate limits"
description: "Rate limiting policies for the Perch Core API"
---

Requests are rate limited on a rolling hourly quota with a burst allowance of up to 6x the hourly average per minute.

For example, if your quota is 500 requests/hour, you cannot exceed 50 requests/minute. The window is rolling — if you exhaust your quota between 07:30 and 07:45, you must wait until 08:30 before making further requests.

## Quotas

Your specific limits depend on your partnership agreement.

| Environment | Typical Quota | Burst Allowance |
|-------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Sandbox | 100–200 requests/hour | Up to 6x the per-minute average |
| Production | Based on your integration needs | Up to 6x the per-minute average |

To find out your quota or request an increase, contact [partnersupport@myperch.io](mailto:partnersupport@myperch.io).
